"Welcome to the Lake Havasu Museum of History. As you walk through the museum, you are journeying through centuries of history, not only of this beautiful city's treasured past, but also the first and sometimes forgotten long-lost inhabitants of this part of the Colorado River area. Your tour will take you through several facets of the region's early and colorful years, the different cultures and peoples who traverse this area, long before the London Bridge was here. You will learn exactly how this city came to life. We hope you enjoy the exhibits!
